In response to the current COVID-19 situation, STTP Archery Academy is taking the following precautions:

In order to protect our students and staff, in-person classes and private lessons are suspended until further notice. When the Academy reopens, it will be in compliance with Washington State guidelines for reopening entertainment centers.

Who We Are

Straight to the Point Archery is a non profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen. Our state of the art educational and training facility incorporates detailed technical video analysis combined with generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors.

Instruction Calendar

We anticipate that regular instruction can resume in June, but class sizes will be limited to ensure sufficient social distancing.
